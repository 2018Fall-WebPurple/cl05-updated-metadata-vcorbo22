Softball is the Best Sport!

There is nothing soft about softball!

Softball is a sport that is exactly like baseball. It is also a great sport to work on teamwork.

Softball is a sport that is very basic, but is fun to play. To play softball you only need: a bat, a glove, a halmet, and a ball. At a time nine players are on the field at one time. There is: one picter, one chatcher, one first baseman, one second baseman, one third baseman, one shortstop, and three outfielders. A softball feild is in the shape of a diamond. The beingnning to the middle of the diamond is dirt. The other part of the diamond is grass.

There are different types of organization in softball. These organization is the league you play for. Must of the time the organization you play for is the name of your team. The best way to figure the organization out is by looking at the name on the front of your shirt. There is also different associations in softball. Associations have different rules sometimes, so when playing the game know the associations you are playing by.
